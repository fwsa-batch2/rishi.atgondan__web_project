TEAM SOUL:-

SouL, an Indian esports organization, revealed its anticipated Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) roster today. The star-studded team features Naman “Mortal” Mathur, Yash “Viper” Soni, Parv “Regaltos” Singh, Tanmay “Sc0utOP” Singh, and Harmandeep “Mavi” Singh

RECKOGNING ESPORTS:-

Reckoning Esports has now turned their attention towards the newest mobile Battle Royale that has been exclusively launched for India - BGMI. Their experienced four-man roster consists of Mohamed "Abrar" Abrar A, Arun "Maxy" Raj R, Shabarish "MrSpray" V, and Kavin "BeardBaba" VM. The lineup used to play under that banner of Walkouts and have spent enough time playing the previous version of the game competitively at the highest level within the country.

TEAM TSM ENTITY:-

TSM Entity's roster featuring Abhijeet “Ghatak” Andhare, Suraj “Neyoo” Majumdar, Jonathan Amaral, Abhishek “ZGOD” Choudhary, and Vivek “ClutchGod” Horo was one of the strongest in the region. The organization announced earlier this month that they have “mutually” decided to part ways.

    RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

    1. All players must install and activate GAC (anti-cheating tool) during qualification or the team WILL BE DISQUALIFIED from the PMCO. See the PMCO Qualification GAC Guideline.

    2. Rosters are finalized upon registration submission.

    3. Participants must have reached 16 years of age or older

    4. For teams of 5, at least 3 players’ nationalities/residents must be from the same region they registered in. For teams of 6, at least 4 players’ nationalities/residents must be from the same region they registered in.

    5. All players must be ranked Platinum or above at the time of registration.

    6. Players are only allowed to use mobile devices during the tournament
