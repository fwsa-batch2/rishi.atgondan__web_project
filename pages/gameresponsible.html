Why Game Responsibly?

We know you love hot-dropping into Erangel, acing those headshots or maybe spending hours trying to improve that finish count, but when you play for hours, you hardly find time to look up at the world. We are here to remind you that the real world is equally exciting too. We at Battlegrounds Mobile India, share the same passion for gaming as you. At the same time we urge you to look-up every now and then from your game as well. Enjoy our games in moderation, maintain a healthy game-life balance and spend quality time in the real world.

Always remember,

TREAT GAMING AS A FORM OF ENTERTAINMENT AND DON’T LET IT OVERPOWER YOUR LIFE. GAME RESPONSIBLY.
