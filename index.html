    RULES AND REGULATIONS:-

    1. All players must install and activate GAC (anti-cheating tool) during qualification or the team WILL BE DISQUALIFIED from the PMCO. See the PMCO Qualification GAC Guideline.

    2. Rosters are finalized upon registration submission.

    3. Participants must have reached 16 years of age or older

    4. For teams of 5, at least 3 players’ nationalities/residents must be from the same region they registered in. For teams of 6, at least 4 players’ nationalities/residents must be from the same region they registered in.

    5. All players must be ranked Platinum or above at the time of registration.

    6. Players are only allowed to use mobile devices during the tournament
